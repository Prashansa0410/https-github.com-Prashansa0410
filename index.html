About Me

I was born in Dehradun and spent my school years across Dehradun and Chennai. My passion for technology began in Classes 11 and 12 when I chose Computer Science and discovered C++.

I completed my B.Tech. in Information Technology from GNIT. After graduating in 2012 without a campus placement, I prepared for AMCAT and eLitmus, earned strong scores, and began my career at Accenture.

My journey continued through Oracle India, GE, and finally Visa Inc., where I spent over six years growing into a Staff Software Engineer building distributed platforms and backend systems for global payment infrastructure.

In 2025, I chose to pursue an M.Tech. in AI & ML at BITS Pilani while spending time with my daughter. Today I maintain a CGPA of 9.52 and build production-inspired AI, cloud and distributed systems projects.

Async Order Processing Platform
In Progress

A production-inspired Java backend simulating how a real-world order processing system evolves from a simple asynchronous workflow into a scalable, concurrent service — built incrementally to demonstrate modern Java concurrency and backend design.

  • Designed an immutable, thread-safe Order domain model and a stateless OrderValidator to eliminate shared mutable state across concurrent requests
  • Built an asynchronous OrderService pipeline using CompletableFuture, applying constructor-based dependency injection for loose coupling and testability
  • Applied core concurrency APIs — supplyAsync, thenApply, thenAccept — and reasoned through Java's internal callback registration and execution model
  • Following SOLID principles and Dependency Inversion throughout; roadmap includes Inventory, Payment, and Fraud Detection services with custom thread pools
